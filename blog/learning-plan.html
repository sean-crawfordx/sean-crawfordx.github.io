🛠️ Bootcamp Learning Plan

I'm especially interested in cybersecurity, but I’m open to other areas too.
I'm using Dev Academy to explore what's possible, develop my confidence, and learn the skills I need to get my foot in the door.

🧑‍🏫 I expect LOTS of patience 😄.

✅ I will share my thoughts and problems, and manage my time and energy with care.
I'll set clear start and stop times, take breaks, move my body, and connect with people outside of Bootcamp.
